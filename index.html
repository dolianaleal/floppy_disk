90's

The 1990s was a decade of great cultural change, and this was reflected in the way people dressed and lived their lives. Street fashion was particularly vibrant in the 90s, with a wide range of styles emerging from different subcultures.

  • Grunge: This style was characterized by loose-fitting clothing, flannel shirts, and Doc Martens boots. It was inspired by the music of bands like Nirvana and Pearl Jam.
  • Hip-hop: This style was characterized by baggy jeans, oversized sweatshirts, and baseball caps. It was inspired by the music of artists like Tupac Shakur and Biggie Smalls.
  • Raver: This style was characterized by bright colors, neon lights, and platform shoes. It was inspired by the rave scene of the early 90s.
  • Sporty: This style was characterized by tracksuits, sneakers, and sportswear brands like Nike and Adidas. It was inspired by the rise of aerobics and other fitness trends in the 90s.
